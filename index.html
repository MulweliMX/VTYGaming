    What You get from ATS in Comparison to ETS2

    American Truck Simulator (ATS) offers a unique trucking experience set in the United States, showcasing diverse landscapes and iconic cities. The game features a distinct American trucking culture, including recognizable trucks and trailers. While Euro Truck Simulator 2 (ETS2) explores the European continent, ATS provides a refreshing perspective with its American scenery, road networks, and trucking challenges. Players can choose between the two games based on their preference for exploring European or American landscapes.
